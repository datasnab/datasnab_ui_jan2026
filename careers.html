Welcome to the career page of Datasnab, where innovation meets opportunity. We are a leading IT company that is always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our team.

At Datasnab, we believe that our employees are our greatest asset. We are committed to creating a positive and supportive work environment where our team members can grow both personally and professionally. We offer competitive salaries, comprehensive health insurance, and opportunities for professional development and advancement.

We are currently hiring for a variety of positions across different departments, including:

DATABASE ADMINISTRATORS:

Install and Configure PostgreSQL database instances on ON-Prem/AWS RDS. Configure the monitoring and alerting of all Postgres DB servers with PEM monitoring. Monitor database performances to identify slowdowns or potential issues. Maintain Database authentication modes, creation of users, and configure permissions for users. Resolve gaps in Data Guard. Apply using incremental RMAN Backup. Rebuild indexes and analyze tables to ensure good performance of the databases. Skills required: PostgreSQL, Oracle, Red hat Linux, Python, Aurora, CloudWatch, SNS, AWS, and Snowflake. Bachelor’s degree in Science, Technology, or Engineering (any) along with 5 years of experience in the job offered or related occupation is required. Work location: San Francisco, CA and various unanticipated locations throughout the U.S.

Email Resume to HR Dept., Datasnab, LLC., at sudheer@datasnab.com

Should the candidate accept employment with Datasnab, LLC., the referring employee will be eligible to receive an award of $1,000.00 for the successful referral.
